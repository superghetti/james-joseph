Wick School of Irish Dance

Celebrating 30 Years of Irish Dance!

Who We Are:

The Wick School promotes advancement of Irish dance. Irish stepdance is a traditional form of dance that people have been enjoying for centuries. A weekly class introduces children to all the basic steps. They will also discover a form of music that they rarely hear on the radio, but is fun, lively, and energetic. It's wholesome, it's social, and it's a great cultural experience!

Instructors and Assistants


Linnane Wick, T.C.R.G.

Linnane has owned and operated the Wick School in Colorado since 1987. She is an Oireachtas champion, placed top 3 at the North American, and has performed extensively with Green Fields of America, Celtic Thunder, and as a member of Cherish the Ladies. Her performances include appearances at The Washington Irish Folk Festival, The Philadelphia Folk Festival, The Milwalkee Irish Fest, The National Folk Festival, and The White House.

Maureen Shea, T.C.R.G.

Maureen began dancing with the Wick School in 1987 soon after Linanne opened her school. She went on to rise through the competitive ranks regionally, nationally and internationally. With numerous world qualifications, a regional title and many places at the national level, Maureen went on to tour with the Trinity Irish Dance Company out of Chicago. She traveled with this company for four years and was lucky enough to see numerous parts of the world including tours in Taiwan, Puerto Rico, Canada and the United States. Maureen has been teaching with the Wick School since 2003 and received her TCRG certification in 2008. Outside of dancing, Maureen is a Realtor, enjoys running, fitness and travel.

Jillian Oury

Jillian Oury Watson started Irish Dancing with the Trinity Academy of Irish Dance at the age of 6 after seeing the dancers practicing on her way out of a gymnastics class at an adjoining gym. She spent many years performing in shows and competing in solo and team competitions with Trinity, ending her solo career with 8 Midwest Regional titles, 4 National titles, 2 All-Ireland titles, and numerous 2nd and 3rd place finishes at the World Championships. She also had the privilege of performing with Trinity on the Today Show, Good Morning America, with the Chicago Symphony Orchestra, and at many other exciting venues. In addition to teaching dance, Jillian also teaches middle school mathematics.
